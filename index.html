SCANLINE IO

3d Scanning | Modeling | Data Processing

Scanline IO provides 3d scanning services and develops software solutions for architectural design projects. We use novel modeling workflows to document existing conditions and develop software tools to better integrate spatial data into the design process.

We work with point cloud data in research and practice to mobilize the depth, speed, and accuracy of the 3d scan in a flexible design modeling environment.
